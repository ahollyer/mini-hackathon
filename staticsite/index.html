FAQ

MINI-HACKATHON
What's so "mini" about it?

The only thing "mini" about it is how we've squeezed all the awesomeness of a 24-hour hackathon into just 12 hours. We offer hackers and volunteers a great event, without giving up a great night's sleep.

WHO CAN ATTEND
Do I have to be a programmer?

Everyone is welcome! This is a beginner-friendly event. If you have a project you're passionate about, come on out, even if you aren't a programmer or designer. Those under 18 must have a parent or guardian present.

WHAT TO BRING
Will there be power strips?

We've got you covered with power strips, good wi-fi, and plenty of food throughout the day. Plan to bring your own electronics: laptops, cables, headphones, etc. Those with dietary restrictions may want to bring their own food.

HOW MUCH TO ATTEND
Is there a fee?

100% free. We'll even give you some freebies for participating, thanks to our generous sponsors! We do welcome any donations and will use the funds to help make this event even better for everyone.

TOOLS & TECH
What software can I use?

You are welcome to use any software you have access to. All project submissions will be made through DevPost, which is free to use.

FINDING A TEAM
Help! I don't know anyone.

Not to worry! You should have no problem finding a team the day of the event.
